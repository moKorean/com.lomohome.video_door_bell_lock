This app brings your RTSP IP cameras, including video doorbells, together with a smart door lock and your motion or doorbell sensors in one door view. Add one or several cameras and watch the live stream at a glance, with no cloud and no transcoding. The app can search your local network to find cameras for you, so in most cases you only need to fill in the stream address once.

A door lock, a doorbell sensor and a motion sensor are all optional, so a camera-only device shows just the video, while a fully linked device also shows sensor status and a lock or unlock button in the same place. From there you can build automations: check the camera and unlock when someone rings, turn on the porch light and start recording on motion, or lock the door automatically at night. Linked devices can be changed later at any time, and the interface follows your language. Everything runs locally and stays under your control.
