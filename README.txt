A video doorbell shows you who is at the door, and a smart lock lets you decide whether to let them in. This app brings the two together so you can see, hear and act on a visitor from one place, at home or away.

When someone rings, you can check the camera, then lock or unlock the door right away, or wire it into automations: turn on the porch light and start a recording when the bell rings, or lock the door automatically at night. Everything runs locally and stays under your control.
